jquery.formatNumber

jQuery plugin for formatting numbers, good for currencies. Option to choose the cents and thousands digit. Defaults to US' 9,999.00. Usage:

$('.number').formatNumber();

This will make <span class="number">2932389423</span> for e.g., be formatted into <span class="number">29,323,894.23</span>
